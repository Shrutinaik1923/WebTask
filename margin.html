margin properties

margins are used to create spaces around elements.there are 4 properties of margin. 1.margin-top 2.margin-right 3.margin-bottom 4.margin-left. the value of these properies can be auto OR length OR % OR inherit. auto means margins will be calculated by the browser. length specifies length of the margins.it can be px,pt,cm. inherit specifies it should be inherited from its parent.
